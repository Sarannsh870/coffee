Our Story

Founded in 2025, Brew Haven has been serving the community with passion for quality coffee.

In the heart of the city, where the morning rush blurs into the quiet hum of afternoon chatter, Brew Haven has been a warm refuge since 2025. Founded by two longtime friends, Mia and saransh, the coffee shop was born from a shared love for rich, carefully crafted coffee and a dream of creating a space where people could pause and connect.

The early days were challenging—opening during a global pandemic meant adapting quickly, from launching a strong takeout model to spreading joy through handwritten notes on every cup. But the community responded with love, and soon, Brew Haven became more than just a café; it became a haven.
